ABOUT


I am an independent level & game designer, creative collaborator, writer and storyteller, with 2 years of professional game industry experience and also 2 years in independent games.

I thrive in creative and collaborative environments where hard work and passion for making great games is valued and where I can leverage my strong technical background to enhance my designs and team.

I strongly believe that, creativity does not come from idleness, rather it must be nurtured and built through a wide set of life experiences.

What Drives Me ?

Learning/Exploring new things like any tools or skills, as they can help in bringing the best out of you. Applying my skills to help my teammates and so that the projects can be designed in the best way as much as possible. Sharing new experiences with friends.
Finding cool solutions to problems and proving that "Yeah, that crazy idea really was possible!"

Whether it be new approaches to game design, or just the release of an anticipated game, it all falls within my love and passion for my medium.
I am enthusiastic and passionate about games, also self motivated about designing the games.
My days are not limited to games, as I take time out of my day to look for inspiration in film, books, art or simply stroll along the harbor.
More about me
I started my career as a Mechanical Engineer and don't actually remember when i got my hands dirty in the field of Game Designing, but i enjoyed the flow. I started with Mario & Contra and these games started waving on my mind all day long.
Playing games and Designing games soon got added to my hobbies and are still at the top of the list.
